# Dependents & Contacts

**Module:** Core HR  
**Feature:** Dependents & Contacts

---

## Purpose

Manages employee dependents (spouse, children, parents) and emergency contact information.

## Database Tables

### `employee_dependents`
Fields: `name`, `relationship` (`spouse`, `child`, `parent`, `other`), `date_of_birth`, `is_emergency_contact`, `phone`.
